FAQ

Why should I work with RexSoft?

RexSoft is a custom software development company with more than 9 years of experience. We have more than hundreds of successful projects for small and midsize businesses. We have over 50 highly skilled professionals, who have experience in custom software development. All this allows us to provide clients with the best service possible, creating technologically advanced, convenient, good-looking software solutions with business value that fully realize the client's idea. You can see RexSoft’s success stories on this page or check out the company’s Clutch page.

Which custom software development services do you offer?

We provide a wide range of custom software development services. Our experience and expertise allow us to be a full-service company and create high-quality turnkey solutions. RexSoft custom software development services include IT consulting, direct web or mobile custom solution development, UX/UI design creation as well as post-release maintenance. In addition, we provide services for the further marketing promotion of the created software product using the most advanced tools.

Which industries do you have experience in?

RexSoft software development company has managed to work with many clients from a variety of industries. That's why we have expertise in creating custom software applications for gaming, logistics and automotive, retail and e-commerce, finance and banking, entertainment, and real estate. Moreover, the list is not limited to these industries.

What is your technology stack?

We work with advanced technological solutions to develop the best custom software possible. Predominantly, we work with Flutter, Unity, JavaScript, SQL, MySQL, PHP, C#, React Native, and Angular.JS.

How long will you support a finished software product?

As long as you need it. Our custom software development company provides several support models to choose from, as well as the possibility to create an exclusive maintenance model on unique conditions.
